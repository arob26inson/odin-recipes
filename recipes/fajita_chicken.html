Fajita Chicken

A quick, delicious, and healthy weeknight dinner

This will give you a protein boost while being easy on the calories

Plus, you'll love the taste so much, you'll forget its healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 table spoons lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper

Steps

  1. Slice Chicken Breasts in half the long way to achieve uniform thickness
  2. Place Chicken Breasts in a bowl, and pour in the olive oil and lime juice
  3. Combine the seasoning and pour into the bowl. Mix the bowl well
  4. Let chicken marinate in the fridge for 10 minutes to 1 dayâ€“ longer is better!
  5. On a high heat pan, place the marinated breasts and cook for 6 minutes
  6. Flip the chicken breasts and cook for another 6 minutes
  7. Flip the chicken breasts every 30 seconds until chicken breast is firm when you poke it
  8. Take the chicken breasts off, and let it rest for 3 minutes
  9. Feast!
